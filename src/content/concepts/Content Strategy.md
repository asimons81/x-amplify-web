# Content Strategy

**Goal:** Automate growth on X and Newsletter.

## The Funnel
1.  **Inspiration:** Automated scraping of TechCrunch/Hacker News (Night Shift).
2.  **Selection:** Tony picks the winners on the Dashboard.
3.  **Drafting:** AI-assisted drafting (Ozzy + Gemini).
4.  **Distribution:**
    -   **X:** High frequency, visual, threads.
    -   **Newsletter:** Weekly summary of the best X posts + deep dive.

## Niche: AI Tools & Workflow
Focus on "How to actually use this stuff to make money/save time," not just "Look at this cool demo."
Target audience: People like Tony (trying to escape 9-5).

## Posting Schedule
- **X:** 2x Daily (Morning/Evening).
- **Newsletter:** Sunday Mornings.
